# Smart Demand Forecasting & Inventory Optimizer

## Abstract

Smart Demand Forecasting & Inventory Optimizer is an AI-powered decision support system that helps retailers and supply chain managers predict future product demand and optimize inventory levels. Using machine learning regression models and inventory optimization techniques, the system reduces stock shortages, minimizes excess inventory, and supports data-driven business decisions.

---

## Keywords

**Artificial Intelligence (AI), Demand Forecasting, Inventory Optimization, Machine Learning, Supply Chain Management, Retail Analytics, Regression Models, Python**

## 1. Introduction

The system assists retailers in forecasting future demand and maintaining optimal inventory levels using AI, helping improve operational efficiency and reduce inventory costs.

## 2. Problem Statement

Retail businesses often struggle with inaccurate demand forecasting, leading to stock shortages, overstocking, and increased operational costs.
